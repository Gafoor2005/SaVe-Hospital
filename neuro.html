Precision Neurosurgical Solutions for Brain, Spine, and Nerve Health

Expert Neuro Surgery Care

SaVe Hospital’s Neuro Surgery Department offers cutting-edge treatments for complex brain, spine, and nervous system conditions. Our team uses advanced microsurgical techniques and neuronavigation to ensure the highest precision.

Whether it’s a brain tumor, spine disorder, or nerve injury, our specialists are trained in the latest minimally invasive procedures to reduce recovery time and improve outcomes.

Meet Our Specialists

Our neurosurgeons have specialized training in brain, spinal cord, and nerve surgeries. They collaborate closely with neurologists, radiologists, and rehabilitation teams to offer holistic care for each patient.

Specialized Procedures:

  • Brain Tumor Surgery
  • Spine Stabilization & Disc Surgery
  • Minimally Invasive Neurosurgery
  • Trauma & Emergency Neurosurgery

State-of-the-Art Facilities

Our neurosurgical operating rooms are equipped with intraoperative MRI, neuronavigation systems, and advanced microscopes to enhance surgical accuracy. We maintain strict sterile protocols and post-operative care standards.

Neuro ICU, rehabilitation services, and imaging diagnostics support the patient throughout their journey to recovery.
