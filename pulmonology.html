Advanced Pulmonology Care

SaVe Hospital's Pulmonology Department is a center of excellence for diagnosing and treating respiratory diseases. Our team of expert pulmonologists is equipped to handle everything from common conditions like asthma and bronchitis to complex disorders such as COPD, pulmonary fibrosis, and interstitial lung disease.

With high-resolution imaging, advanced pulmonary function testing, and minimally invasive procedures like bronchoscopy, we ensure a precise diagnosis and a tailored treatment plan for every patient.

Meet Our Specialists

At SaVe Hospital, our pulmonologists bring global expertise and years of clinical experience to treat respiratory illnesses with accuracy and compassion. From outpatient consultations to critical care management in the ICU, we follow internationally accepted guidelines and protocols.

Key Expertise:

  • Asthma & Allergy Management
  • Sleep Apnea Diagnosis & Treatment
  • Bronchoscopy & Lung Biopsy
  • Ventilator & ICU Support

World-Class Facilities

Our department is equipped with a modern Pulmonary Function Test (PFT) lab, sleep study rooms, high-end ventilators, and non-invasive respiratory support tools. The combination of technology, infrastructure, and expert care ensures patients get the most effective and safest treatments.

We also offer smoking cessation programs and rehabilitation for chronic lung conditions, helping patients achieve long-term respiratory health and an improved quality of life.
