Critical Care Department - SaVe Hospital

Life-Saving Support with 24/7 Intensive Monitoring and Advanced Technology

24x7 Intensive Care

The Critical Care Department at SaVe Hospital is equipped to provide round-the-clock monitoring and life-saving treatment for patients with life-threatening conditions. Our ICUs are staffed with trained intensivists, critical care nurses, and advanced life support technologies.

We follow international protocols in managing patients with severe infections, trauma, post-operative complications, and multi-organ failure, ensuring the highest standards of care and safety.

Our Critical Care Team

Our multidisciplinary team of experts includes intensivists, anesthesiologists, surgeons, pulmonologists, and emergency physicians. Together, they provide collaborative care tailored to each patient’s condition.

Our Focus Areas:

  • Advanced ICU & Ventilator Management
  • Sepsis and Shock Treatment
  • Post-Surgical Critical Monitoring
  • Emergency Trauma Support

Cutting-Edge Facilities

Our critical care units feature ventilators, dialysis units, central oxygen systems, bedside ultrasound, and patient monitors with real-time data. Infection control, 24x7 diagnostics, and rapid response ensure optimal outcomes.

We also provide family counseling and psychological support for patients and caregivers to help them through the intensive care journey.
