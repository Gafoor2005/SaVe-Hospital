Comprehensive Surgical Services

SaVe Hospital’s General Surgery Department offers a wide spectrum of surgical services—from minor outpatient procedures to complex operations. We utilize the latest medical technologies and follow strict protocols to ensure patient safety, faster recovery, and minimal complications.

Our surgical services cover gastrointestinal, endocrine, breast, hernia, and skin-related conditions, handled with precision and care.

Skilled Surgeons

Our highly trained surgeons combine clinical excellence with years of hands-on experience to deliver optimal surgical outcomes. We prioritize patient comfort, safety, and recovery through personalized treatment plans.

Key Surgical Expertise:

  • Laparoscopic (Minimally Invasive) Surgery
  • Gallbladder & Appendix Surgery
  • Hernia Repairs (Inguinal, Umbilical, Hiatal)
  • Breast Lumps & Abscesses

Modern Facilities

Our surgical suites are equipped with state-of-the-art operation theaters, advanced imaging systems, and sterilization technology. SaVe Hospital ensures that all procedures meet international standards in safety and efficiency.

From pre-surgical evaluations to post-op recovery rooms, every aspect of care is designed to offer comfort, reduce stress, and promote healing.
