Neurology Department - SaVe Hospital

Precision Diagnosis & Advanced Care for Brain, Spine & Nerve Disorders

Expert Neurological Treatment

SaVe Hospital's Neurology Department offers comprehensive evaluation and treatment of brain, spine, and nerve-related disorders. Our highly skilled neurologists use cutting-edge diagnostic tools such as EEG, EMG, MRI, and CT scans to diagnose even the most complex neurological conditions.

We specialize in managing stroke, epilepsy, Parkinson's disease, multiple sclerosis, and other neurodegenerative disorders with personalized care plans and evidence-based medicine.

Meet Our Neurologists

Our team comprises board-certified neurologists with extensive experience in diagnosing and treating a wide spectrum of neurological diseases. With a multidisciplinary approach and access to ICU and stroke units, we ensure rapid and effective treatment.

Conditions We Treat:

  • Epilepsy & Seizure Disorders
  • Stroke & Transient Ischemic Attacks (TIAs)
  • Parkinson's & Movement Disorders
  • Neuropathy & Nerve Compression Syndromes
  • Headache & Migraine Management

World-Class Neuro Facilities

The Neurology wing is supported by an advanced Neuro ICU, 24/7 neuroimaging services, and interventional neurology capabilities. We ensure swift diagnosis and emergency care, especially for stroke and trauma patients.

Our commitment to technology, patient safety, and comfort makes SaVe Hospital a preferred destination for neurological care.
