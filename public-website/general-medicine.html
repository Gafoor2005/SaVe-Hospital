General Medicine Department - SaVe Hospital

Comprehensive Primary Care for Adults with Compassion and Expertise

Comprehensive Medical Services

At SaVe Hospital, the General Medicine Department is your first point of contact for adult healthcare. We specialize in the prevention, diagnosis, and non-surgical treatment of various conditions including infections, diabetes, hypertension, and metabolic disorders.

Our internists are trained to provide continuous care and manage chronic diseases with a holistic approach, ensuring every patient receives personalized and evidence-based treatment.

Our Expertise

Our general physicians work in collaboration with specialists across other departments to ensure seamless and coordinated care. They’re also trained in emergency and critical care management.

Key Areas We Cover:

  • Fever and Infectious Diseases
  • Diabetes & Hypertension Management
  • Thyroid and Hormonal Disorders
  • Preventive Health Checkups

Integrated Facilities

We provide diagnostic labs, ECG, imaging, and pharmacy services under one roof to make your healthcare experience smooth and efficient. Our in-patient services include dedicated wards for patients needing continuous observation.

From health counseling to lifestyle modification advice, our department ensures holistic care for long-term well-being.
