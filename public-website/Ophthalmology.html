Cataract Treatment & Surgery

Cataracts are a common age-related condition where the natural lens of the eye becomes cloudy, leading to blurry vision, glare, and difficulty seeing at night.

At SaVe Hospital, we offer advanced diagnostic tools and cutting-edge cataract surgery with intraocular lens implants for crystal-clear vision.

Meet Dr. Praveena

Dr. Praveena is a leading ophthalmologist known for her meticulous surgical skills and patient-focused care. She performs minimally invasive cataract surgeries that ensure quick recovery and improved quality of life.

Glaucoma Diagnosis & Management

Glaucoma is a silent thief of sight, often progressing without symptoms until significant damage occurs. It affects the optic nerve due to increased eye pressure.

At SaVe Hospital, we utilize state-of-the-art diagnostic techniques like OCT and tonometry for early detection and prevention of vision loss.

Vision Preservation Expert

With a focus on preserving vision, Dr. Praveena tailors each glaucoma treatment plan—ranging from eye drops to laser therapy and surgery—based on the patient’s condition and lifestyle.

Diabetic Retinopathy Care

Diabetic Retinopathy is a diabetes-related condition that damages the blood vessels in the retina, leading to blurred vision, dark spots, and potential blindness.

At SaVe Hospital, we provide early detection using advanced retinal imaging and personalized treatment including laser therapy and injections.

Precision Eye Care

Dr. Praveena is skilled in managing diabetic eye disease with a comprehensive, compassionate approach that helps protect vision and quality of life for diabetic patients.
