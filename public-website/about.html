About SaVe Hospital

Located in the heart of Machilipatnam, SaVe Hospital is a leading multispeciality center delivering excellence across various medical fields. With a patient-first approach, internationally trained doctors, and world-class infrastructure, we are committed to transforming healthcare.

Our hospital blends compassion with innovation—offering comprehensive care through state-of-the-art facilities and dedicated departments that ensure every patient receives tailored and effective treatment. SaVe Hospital is more than a healthcare provider—it's a trusted partner in your well-being.

ENT – Ear, Nose & Throat Care

Our ENT specialists, led by renowned experts, offer advanced care for sinusitis, hearing loss, tonsillitis, and more. With endoscopic sinus surgery, audiology labs, and image-guided tools, patients receive accurate diagnoses and effective treatments in a comfortable environment.

Ophthalmology – Vision & Eye Care

From routine eye checkups to cataract surgery and LASIK, our eye care department provides cutting-edge vision correction. Equipped with advanced microscopes and retinal diagnostics, we restore and protect vision with precision and care.

Pulmonology – Advanced Lung Care

Our pulmonologists specialize in treating asthma, COPD, sleep apnea, and respiratory infections. With pulmonary function testing labs, bronchoscopy units, and personalized rehab programs, we breathe life into better lung health.

Neurology – Brain & Nerve Excellence

The Neurology team at SaVe Hospital excels in diagnosing and treating epilepsy, stroke, migraine, and neurodegenerative disorders. Our neurologists utilize advanced MRI, EEG, and neuro rehab technologies for early intervention and holistic care.

General Medicine – Holistic Internal Care

Our General Medicine department is the backbone of SaVe Hospital. From infections to lifestyle diseases like diabetes and hypertension, our internists provide evidence-based, comprehensive care with a preventive focus.

Radiology – Precision Imaging

Accurate diagnosis begins with imaging. Our Radiology department features advanced digital X-rays, MRI, CT scans, and ultrasound facilities. With rapid reporting and AI-integrated systems, we support all departments in timely, detailed analysis.
