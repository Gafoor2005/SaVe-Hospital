Expert Sinusitis Treatment at SaVe Hospital

Sinusitis is a condition where the tissue lining the sinuses becomes inflamed, leading to symptoms like facial pain, nasal congestion, pressure headaches, and loss of smell. If untreated, it can severely affect quality of life.

At SaVe Hospital, we specialize in diagnosing and treating both acute and chronic sinusitis using the latest medical and surgical techniques.

He utilizes advanced diagnostics, image-guided functional endoscopic sinus surgery (FESS), and custom treatment plans to help patients recover fully and quickly. His patient-first approach, combined with technological precision, makes him a trusted name in ENT care.

Hearing Loss Diagnosis & Care

Hearing loss affects people of all ages and may result from infections, aging, noise exposure, or structural issues. It can cause difficulty in communication, social isolation, and reduced quality of life.

At SaVe Hospital, we provide precise diagnosis through audiometry, tympanometry, and otoacoustic testing. Our ENT team is skilled in offering personalized care to restore and protect your hearing.

Expert Hearing Restoration

Our treatments range from advanced hearing aids and ear surgeries to implantable hearing solutions. With compassionate care and cutting-edge tools, we help you reconnect with the world of sound.

Tonsillitis Treatment & Relief

Tonsillitis is a condition marked by inflammation of the tonsils, often due to viral or bacterial infections. It can cause severe sore throat, difficulty in swallowing, fever, and swollen lymph nodes.

At SaVe Hospital, we provide accurate diagnosis and personalized treatment plans under the guidance of Dr. Sandeep Vemu, a renowned ENT specialist known for his expertise in managing recurrent and chronic tonsillitis cases.

Advanced ENT Care

Whether you need conservative treatment with antibiotics or advanced surgical intervention like tonsillectomy, Dr. Vemu ensures the safest and most effective outcomes using modern techniques and patient-centered care.
