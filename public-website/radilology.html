Advanced Radiology Imaging

SaVe Hospital’s Radiology Department is equipped with the latest medical imaging technologies to provide accurate diagnostics that guide effective treatment. From routine X-rays to complex MRI scans, our expert team ensures timely and precise imaging.

We are committed to reducing patient wait times, increasing diagnostic accuracy, and maintaining the highest safety standards through low-dose radiation equipment and expert interpretation.

Meet Our Team

Our radiologists and technicians specialize in high-resolution diagnostic imaging and work closely with clinicians to deliver accurate interpretations. We prioritize patient comfort and clarity in communication at every step.

Services Include:

  • MRI, CT Scan & Ultrasound
  • Digital X-ray & Mammography
  • Image-Guided Procedures
  • Interventional Radiology

World-Class Facilities

Our imaging suites are equipped with state-of-the-art radiological systems, including 3T MRI, 128-slice CT, and digital imaging. Every scan is reviewed by certified radiologists and integrated seamlessly into the patient’s care journey.

We also provide emergency radiology support, outpatient services, and rapid reporting capabilities to enhance hospital-wide diagnostics.
